> Archivo: Inteligencia Artificial

This entry details the final project of the subject, consisting of the development of a GOAP (Goal Oriented Action Planning) system in Unity. In this simulation, several autonomous agents plan and execute actions in real-time within a scene to achieve their goals. For this practical case, I created a cemetery-themed simulation, where different entities interact with each other systematically.




[01] Agent Definition

> Gravediggers

They are in charge of digging graves to extract corpses. When their energy runs out, they go to the cabins to rest and recover.

> Necromancers

They collect the corpses unearthed by the Gravediggers and transport them to the altars to summon skeletons. Their resting place is the crypts.

> Skeletons

Once summoned, their only objective is to chase the Gravediggers to finish them off.

> Hunters

They are in charge of maintaining order, hunting down any skeleton that appears in the scene. They rest in the cabins when they get tired.

> Ghosts

Their function is to revive murdered Gravediggers through statues (with a maximum limit of 5 active in the scene). If there is no one to revive, they wander around the cemetery.




[02] Planned Actions (GOAP)

[Dig Grave] Gravediggers' action. They locate a grave, move towards it, and proceed to dig.

[Rest] Common rest action for Gravediggers, Necromancers, and Hunters. The agent locates their assigned building and enters it to recover energy.

[Collect Body] Necromancers' action to pick up an available body and transport it to the altar.

[Dark Ritual] Action executed by Necromancers at the altar to consume the body and generate a skeleton.

[Kill] Offensive action of the skeletons. They search for a nearby Gravedigger and attack until eliminating them.

[Hunt Monster] Hunters' action. They detect skeletons in the scene, chase them, and hunt them down.

[Haunt] Passive action of the ghost to wander randomly around the 'NavMesh Surface' when there are no pending resurrection tasks.

[Resurrect] Priority action of the ghosts to bring fallen Gravediggers back to life.
